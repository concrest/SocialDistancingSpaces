What's this for?

The map shows spaces that are colour coded based on the following:

Green

These spaces are very easy to maintain a good social distancing. They are open spaces, or paths wide enough to pass each other with little to no risk to yourself, your family, and others

Amber

These spaces might be a little risky for maintaining a good social distance, for example pavements or narrow paths with few passing places. It may be awkward to pass each other safely

Red

These spaces are difficult or risky to maintain a good social distance, for example narrow pavements with little or no visibility of oncoming traffic. Approach these places with caution
